## ‚ö° IMMEDIATE ACTIONS (Do These TODAY)

### 1. Set Up Google Business Profile (FREE - Most Important!)
This is the #1 thing that will get you found locally.

**Go to:** https://business.google.com/create

**Fill out EVERYTHING:**
- Business name: Nexera Technology and Consultancy
- Category: "Business Consultant" (primary) + "Web Designer" + "Data Analytics Consultant"
- Address: Your Mississauga address (or set as service-area business)
- Phone number: Your business phone
- Website: Your actual live URL
- Hours: Set business hours
- Description: Use keywords naturally - "Nexera provides Excel automation, Power BI dashboards, Shopify development, and data services for small businesses in Mississauga, Toronto, and the GTA."

**Add these services in Google Business:**
- Excel Automation Services
- Power BI Dashboard Development
- Shopify Store Development
- Website Design
- Data Cleanup Services
- Business Consulting

**Upload photos:**
- Your headshot (builds trust)
- Screenshot of a dashboard you built
- Your workspace/office
- Before/after of a project

**Post weekly updates** (this tells Google you're active):
- "Just completed a Power BI dashboard for a Toronto restaurant"
- "New blog post: How Excel automation saves 10 hours/week"
- Share tips, offers, anything relevant

### 2. Submit Your Sitemap to Google Search Console

**Go to:** Google Search Console ‚Üí Sitemaps

**Submit:** `https://YOUR-DOMAIN.com/sitemap.xml`

Click "Submit" and wait for Google to crawl it.
